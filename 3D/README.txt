Software used for modeling: Autodesk Inventor Professional 2019

You will find .ipt/.iam which are the Autodesk formats, and in order to reach a wider audience, .stl is provided.
The .3mf for printing with Prusa printers is also provided.

Printing Recommendations:
PETG - 40% infill (star) - 0.3mm print bed (0.35mm for the first)
> Supports on the printing bed only
> Pay attention to the tolerance and precision of your printer! (Otherwise the parts will not fit and you will have to sand)
